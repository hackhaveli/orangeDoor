Our Focus

Multifamily Investments

We target properties with below market rents, deferred maintenance, or inefficient management. We create value through renovations and operational efficiency.

Senior Living Communities

We focus on Independent Living, Assisted Living, and Memory Care in high demand markets. We partner with proven operators who deliver quality care.

Other Commercial Investments

Mobile Home Parks, Self Storage, and Flex Industrial Spaces are considered when the numbers and strategy make sense.
